Just as you wouldn't use a flimsy lock to secure your home, it's crucial to create a strong password for your online accounts to protect your personal information.

Here are some tips to help you create a strong and secure password:

  • Use a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.
  • Make your password at least 12 characters long. The longer, the better!
  • Avoid using common phrases or predictable patterns, like "password123" or "qwerty".
  • Do not use personal information, like your name, birthday, or passport number, in your password.
  • Try to use a different password for each of your online accounts.
